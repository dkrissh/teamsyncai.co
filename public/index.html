Most companies hire
the same broken way.
AI can fix that.

Post a job. Get 80 resumes. Schedule 20 screening calls.
Cancel half of them. Hire someone. Hope for the best.
Repeat in 6 months when it doesn't work out.

The problem isn't finding candidates. It's everything that happens before and after — knowing what to actually ask, how to evaluate fairly, and whether the hire worked out the way you hoped.

That's the part nobody teaches you. And it's the part TeamSyncAI is built to solve.

1
Before the interview
We start by asking you the hard questions.

Not about the job description — about what actually makes someone succeed or fail on your specific team. What does good look like at 90 days? What kind of person always flames out here? How much hand-holding is realistic in the first month?

Most founders have never been asked these questions before an interview. Our AI uses your answers to build everything else — calibrated to your team, not a generic template.

2
During the interview
Then we build your interview system.

Not a list of generic questions you found on Google. Evaluation goals weighted to what actually matters for this role. Behavioural questions designed around the answers you gave us. Follow-up probes for when candidates get vague.

The AI generates scoring criteria so you evaluate every candidate the same way — not going with your gut on a Tuesday afternoon because the person seemed nice.

3
After the hire
Then we help you find out if you were right.

Most hiring tools stop at the offer letter. We send you check-in questions at 30, 60, and 90 days — tied to the exact goals you set at the start. Is this person doing what you hired them to do? Are the early warning signs you identified showing up?

Over time, the AI learns what good hiring looks like for your team specifically. Every hire makes the next one smarter.
